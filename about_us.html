ABOUT US

Omnicom CR Enterprises (OCRE) is an innovative company specializing in providing comprehensive solutions in construction, energy efficiency, renewable energy, general electricity, air conditioning, plumbing and sanitation, cleaning, maintenance and gardening, and specialized personnel supply. Founded by experts with over 15 years of experience in both Colombia and the United States, Omnicom combines local and international knowledge to deliver high-quality services and customized solutions to its clients.

MISSION

Our mission is to empower communities and businesses through innovative and sustainable solutions, improving quality of life and promoting a greener environment.

VISION

We aspire to be leaders in the sector, recognized for our ability to deliver high-quality projects that integrate technology, sustainability, and social responsibility, expanding both nationally and internationally.
